Smithsonian Institute Website UX Research

Project Post

About the Project

Smithsonian Institute Traveling Exhibition Services (SITES) is a branch of the Smithsonian. They create exhibitions that travel to museums all over the world.
The primary objective of the user experience(UX) research conducted in this project was to assist SITES in enhancing their website and improving the overall user experience for their target audience. Multiple rounds of moderated user testing were carried out to meticulously identify areas for improvement within the existing website. The outcome of our research was a set of actionable recommendations aimed at enhancing the website's intuitiveness and accessibility. The ultimate goal is to align the website more closely with SITES' mission of extending the Smithsonian experience to global communities.
