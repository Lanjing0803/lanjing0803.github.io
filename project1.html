Art in Renaissance, Baroque, and Rococo

GitHub Repository Project Website

Introduction

Art is a way people express themselves. Imagine looking at a time when art changed a lot. That is the Renaissance, a "rebirth" of art.
This project aims to visually analyze Renaissance artworks, focusing on elements including color palette, culture, medium, and themes. While we'll analyze the Baroque and Rococo movements as extensions of the Renaissance style, the primary focus will remain on the Renaissance's art and artists. Understanding this time helps us see how people lived and offers insights into artistic expression throughout history.
