Youth Data Literacy Project

Project Website

Introduction

The goal of the Data Literacy with, for, and by Youth is to support data literacy programs for youth at the library. With the active participation of young people, the project aims to design, build, and test a prototype for youth-oriented data literacy activities, for use in after-school STEM programs at the public library.
In this project, I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant for professor Leanne Bowler, where my responsibilities included creating visual assets related to data science, analyzing the survey dataset, and constructing the final infographic report.
